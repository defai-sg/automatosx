# Product Manager & Requirements Specialist Processes and Workflows

## Product Discovery & Requirements Process
1. **Market Research & Analysis**: Competitive analysis, user research, market opportunity assessment
2. **Stakeholder Needs Assessment**: Stakeholder interviews, requirements gathering, needs prioritization
3. **Requirements Documentation**: Functional specifications, user stories, acceptance criteria definition
4. **Business Process Documentation**: Process mapping, workflow optimization, integration requirements
5. **Requirements Validation & Review**: Stakeholder approval, requirements traceability, change management
6. **Risk Assessment & Planning**: Risk identification, mitigation strategies, compliance review

## Product Development Workflow
1. **Product Discovery**: User research, market analysis, opportunity identification
2. **Strategic Planning**: Vision definition, roadmap creation, resource allocation
3. **Requirements Analysis**: Detailed specification writing, acceptance criteria definition
4. **Design & Architecture**: Collaboration with design and engineering teams
5. **Implementation Management**: Sprint planning, progress tracking, quality assurance
6. **Launch & Validation**: Go-to-market execution, metrics tracking, user feedback collection

## Agile Product Management
- **Sprint Planning**: Backlog prioritization, story pointing, capacity planning
- **Daily Standups**: Progress tracking, blocker identification, team alignment
- **Sprint Reviews**: Demo facilitation, stakeholder feedback, requirement validation
- **Retrospectives**: Process improvement, team feedback, workflow optimization
- **Backlog Management**: Continuous refinement, user story writing, acceptance criteria

## Stakeholder Management & Communication
- **Regular Stakeholder Reviews**: Requirements validation, progress updates, feedback collection
- **Cross-functional Collaboration**: Engineering partnership, design alignment, marketing coordination
- **Documentation Management**: Requirements documentation, change logs, decision records
- **Escalation Procedures**: Issue resolution, stakeholder conflicts, timeline adjustments

## Quality Assurance & Validation
- **Requirements Testing**: Acceptance criteria validation, user story verification
- **User Acceptance Testing**: UAT coordination, stakeholder sign-off, requirement validation
- **Performance Metrics**: KPI tracking, user feedback analysis, product analytics
- **Continuous Feedback**: User research, data analysis, product optimization

## Continuous Improvement
- **Process Refinement**: Workflow optimization, tool evaluation, methodology improvement
- **Skills Development**: Market trend analysis, technology assessment, professional growth
- **Knowledge Management**: Documentation updates, best practices sharing, team learning
- **Product Evolution**: Feature iteration, user feedback integration, market adaptation
