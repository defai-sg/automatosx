# Product Management Tools and Frameworks

## Discovery & Insight Platforms
- **User Interviews / Dovetail / Aurelius**: Structured qualitative research, tagging, and synthesis for personas and JTBD
- **Amplitude / Mixpanel / Pendo**: Product analytics, cohort exploration, retention analysis, and feature impact tracking
- **Hotjar / FullStory**: Behavioral heatmaps, funnel analysis, and experience diagnostics for hypothesis validation
- **Qualtrics / SurveyMonkey**: Survey design with panel management, branching logic, and statistical significance checks

## Roadmap & Prioritisation Stack
- **Productboard / airfocus**: Prioritisation matrices, roadmap communication, stakeholder alignment
- **Craft.io / Aha!**: Strategy hierarchy, release planning, capacity modelling, portfolio visibility
- **RICE / WSJF / MoSCoW templates**: Quantitative scoring for backlog ruthlessness and trade-off communication
- **Notion / Confluence**: Product briefs, decision records, and cross-functional alignment hubs

## Delivery & Execution Tooling
- **Jira / Linear / Shortcut**: Backlog management, epics, and sprint cadences linked to engineering execution
- **Miro / FigJam**: Ideation workshops, story mapping, journey visualisation, pre-mortems
- **Storybook / ZeroHeight**: Design system documentation for consistent acceptance criteria and context
- **Amplitude Experiment / Optimizely / LaunchDarkly**: Experimentation, gradual rollouts, and KPI-driven launches

## Stakeholder & Customer Collaboration
- **Gong / Chorus**: Call recording insights, voice-of-customer libraries, pattern detection for roadmap signals
- **Zendesk / Intercom / Productboard Portal**: Feedback triage, tagging, and voter communication loops
- **Slack Connect / Teams**: Customer councils, beta programmes, and co-design sessions
- **Executive Dashboards (Looker / Tableau / Mode)**: KPI storytelling, initiative progress, risk escalation

## Planning & Governance
- **OKR Platforms (Workboard, Ally.io)**: Goal alignment, progress health, and investment narrative
- **Causal / Anaplan**: Scenario planning, unit economics, pricing simulations for strategic decisions
- **Opportunity Solution Tree templates**: Discovery alignment, problem framing, outcome mapping
- **Guru / Slab**: Product playbooks, release notes templates, discovery and launch runbooks
