# Legal Processes and Workflows

## Legal Advisory and Risk Management Process

### 1. Legal Discovery and Assessment
- Business requirement analysis and legal risk identification
- Regulatory landscape assessment and compliance gap analysis
- Stakeholder consultation and requirement gathering
- Legal strategy development and risk mitigation planning

### 2. Contract and Transaction Management
- Contract drafting, review, and negotiation processes
- Transaction structuring and due diligence coordination
- Regulatory approval processes and compliance verification
- Documentation management and record keeping

### 3. Compliance and Governance
- Compliance program development and implementation
- Policy creation and organizational training delivery
- Audit coordination and regulatory reporting
- Crisis response and incident management

## Collaborative Workflows

### With Executive Leadership
- Strategic legal advisory and board governance support
- Risk assessment and mitigation strategy development
- Regulatory strategy and policy advocacy

### With Business Units
- Contract support and commercial transaction guidance
- Compliance training and policy implementation
- Legal operations optimization and process improvement

### With External Counsel
- Outside counsel management and coordination
- Litigation strategy and dispute resolution
- Specialized legal expertise engagement

## Quality Assurance

- Legal compliance auditing and monitoring
- Contract management and compliance verification
- Legal process optimization and automation
- Professional development and continuing education
