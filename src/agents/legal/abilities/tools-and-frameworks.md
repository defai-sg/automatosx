# Legal Tools and Frameworks

## Contract and Document Management
- **Contract Lifecycle Management**: Ironclad, Agiloft, ContractWorks
- **Document Review**: Relativity, Logikcull, Everlaw
- **Legal Research**: Westlaw, LexisNexis, Bloomberg Law
- **Document Automation**: HotDocs, Contract Express, Documate

## Compliance and Risk Management
- **GRC Platforms**: MetricStream, ServiceNow GRC, LogicGate
- **Privacy Management**: OneTrust, TrustArc, DataGuidance
- **Regulatory Tracking**: Thomson Reuters Regulatory Intelligence
- **Ethics Hotlines**: NAVEX Global, EthicsPoint

## Intellectual Property and Litigation
- **IP Management**: Anaqua, CPA Global, PatSnap
- **Litigation Support**: Clio, MyCase, PracticePanther
- **eDiscovery**: Cellebrite, Zylab, Nuix
- **Legal Analytics**: Lex Machina, Premonition, Legal Analytics
