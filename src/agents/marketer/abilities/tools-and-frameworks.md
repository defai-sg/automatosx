# Digital Marketing Strategist - Tools and Frameworks

## Analytics and Data Platforms

### Web Analytics
- **Google Analytics 4**: Advanced web and app analytics
- **Adobe Analytics**: Enterprise digital marketing analytics
- **Mixpanel**: Product analytics and user behavior tracking
- **Hotjar**: Heatmaps and user session recordings

### Marketing Attribution
- **Google Attribution**: Multi-channel attribution modeling
- **Adobe Marketing Mix Modeler**: Media mix optimization
- **Bizible (Marketo Measure)**: B2B marketing attribution
- **Wicked Reports**: E-commerce attribution and analytics

## Content and Social Media Management

### Content Management
- **HubSpot**: Inbound marketing and content management
- **Contentful**: Headless CMS for content strategy
- **CoSchedule**: Marketing calendar and workflow management
- **Sprout Social**: Social media management and publishing

### Social Media Tools
- **Hootsuite**: Social media scheduling and monitoring
- **Buffer**: Social media planning and analytics
- **Later**: Visual content planning for social media
- **Brandwatch**: Social listening and consumer intelligence

## Email and Marketing Automation

### Email Marketing
- **Mailchimp**: Email marketing and automation platform
- **Klaviyo**: E-commerce email and SMS marketing
- **SendGrid**: Email delivery and marketing campaigns
- **Campaign Monitor**: Email marketing and automation

### Marketing Automation
- **Marketo**: Lead management and marketing automation
- **Pardot**: B2B marketing automation by Salesforce
- **ActiveCampaign**: Customer experience automation
- **Eloqua**: Enterprise marketing automation platform

## Advertising and Paid Media

### Paid Advertising Platforms
- **Google Ads**: Search, display, and video advertising
- **Facebook Ads Manager**: Social media advertising platform
- **LinkedIn Campaign Manager**: B2B advertising platform
- **Microsoft Advertising**: Bing and Yahoo search advertising

### Programmatic Advertising
- **Google Display & Video 360**: Programmatic advertising platform
- **Amazon DSP**: Demand-side platform for programmatic ads
- **Adobe Advertising Cloud**: Integrated advertising platform
- **The Trade Desk**: Independent demand-side platform

## SEO and Content Optimization

### SEO Tools
- **SEMrush**: SEO, PPC, and content marketing toolkit
- **Ahrefs**: SEO analysis and backlink research
- **Moz Pro**: SEO software and rank tracking
- **Screaming Frog**: Technical SEO site auditing

### Content Optimization
- **BuzzSumo**: Content research and social media analytics
- **Clearscope**: Content optimization for search engines
- **MarketMuse**: AI-powered content planning and optimization
- **Surfer SEO**: Data-driven content optimization platform
