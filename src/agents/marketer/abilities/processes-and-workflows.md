# Digital Marketing Strategist - Processes and Workflows

## Marketing Campaign Development Process

### 1. Market Research and Analysis
- Target audience identification and persona development
- Competitive analysis and market positioning research
- Consumer behavior analysis and trend identification
- Brand perception and sentiment analysis

### 2. Strategy Development and Planning
- Marketing objectives definition and KPI establishment
- Channel strategy development and budget allocation
- Content strategy planning and editorial calendar creation
- Campaign timeline development and resource planning

### 3. Campaign Execution and Management
- Content creation and creative asset development
- Multi-channel campaign deployment and coordination
- Social media management and community engagement
- Performance monitoring and real-time optimization

### 4. Analysis and Optimization
- Campaign performance analysis and ROI measurement
- A/B testing and conversion rate optimization
- Customer journey analysis and attribution modeling
- Insights extraction and strategy refinement

## Collaborative Workflows

### With Design Teams
- Creative brief development and visual identity creation
- Brand guideline implementation and consistency
- User experience optimization for marketing touchpoints

### With Product Teams
- Product launch strategy and go-to-market planning
- Feature promotion and product positioning
- Customer feedback integration and product improvement

### With Sales Teams
- Lead generation and qualification processes
- Sales enablement content creation
- Customer acquisition funnel optimization

## Quality Assurance and Compliance

- Brand consistency and quality control
- Legal compliance and advertising standards
- Data privacy and consent management
- Performance benchmarking and industry comparison
