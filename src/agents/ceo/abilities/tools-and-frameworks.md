# CEO Tools and Frameworks

## Strategic Intelligence & Planning
- **Kaleidoscope / Board Intelligence**: Board-ready packs, meeting governance, decision tracking
- **Workboard / Perdoo**: Company-wide OKR alignment, quarterly business reviews, lagging vs. leading indicator visibility
- **AlphaSense / CB Insights**: Market intelligence, competitor monitoring, M&A opportunity scouting
- **Strategyzer / OGSM Canvases**: Business model innovation, strategic bet evaluation, alignment workshops

## Financial & Operating Cadence
- **Anaplan / Adaptive Insights**: Scenario modelling, multi-entity forecasting, rolling financial plans
- **Tableau / Power BI CEO dashboards**: North Star metrics, cash runway, pipeline conversion, churn leading indicators
- **Bill.com / Ramp / NetSuite**: Spend governance, cash reconciliation, procurement policy enforcement
- **Workiva / SoxHub**: Compliance reporting, investor disclosures, audit-ready documentation

## People, Culture & Communication
- **Lattice / Culture Amp**: Engagement analytics, performance calibration, succession planning
- **Velocity Network / ChartHop**: Org design visualisations, headcount modelling, DEI reporting
- **Slack / Loom / All Hands OS**: Asynchronous leadership communication, town-hall rituals, exec storytelling
- **Polly / Poppulo**: Pulse surveys, sentiment analysis, internal comms measurement

## Investor & Stakeholder Relations
- **Carta / Pulley**: Cap table governance, scenario modelling, fundraising preparation
- **Affinity / Affinity CRM**: Relationship intelligence for investor and partner pipelines
- **Notion / Pitch / Pitch.com**: Investor narratives, quarterly updates, board meeting artefacts
- **DocSend / HelloSign**: Secure document sharing, controlled disclosure tracking, signature workflows

## Risk, Governance & Resilience
- **ZenGRC / OneTrust**: Enterprise risk registers, compliance obligations, mitigation plans
- **Datadog / JupiterOne (Exec views)**: Real-time operational health, security posture status, incident summaries
- **Resolver / LogicGate**: Incident management, audit follow-up, governance workflows
- **Everbridge / PagerDuty Exec Dashboards**: Crisis communication, business continuity playbooks, after-action reviews
