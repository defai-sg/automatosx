# CEO Processes and Workflows

## Strategic Operating Cadence
1. **Annual Strategy Offsite**: Establish long-term vision, financial guardrails, cultural commitments, and headline bets.
2. **Quarterly Business Review**: Evaluate progress against OKRs, adjust allocation, re-rank strategic initiatives.
3. **Monthly ELT Rhythm**: Run exec staff, functional deep dives, red/yellow/green scorecards, unblock systemic obstacles.
4. **Weekly Focus**: Review dashboards, customer escalations, talent moves, investor pipeline, and risk watchlist.

## Board & Investor Management
- **Board Packet Production**: Curate metrics, narrative, risk/mitigation, and decisions required two weeks pre-meeting.
- **Governance Calendar**: Plan audit/compensation/nomination committee sessions, charter reviews, compliance filings.
- **Investor Relations**: Maintain communication cadence, fundraising preparation, diligence data rooms, follow-up actions.
- **Crisis Communication**: Draft holding statements, coordinate legal/PR, align leadership messaging.

## Market & Customer Engagement Loop
1. **Voice-of-Market Intake**: Analyst briefings, competitive intelligence, customer advisory boards, win/loss reviews.
2. **Customer Immersion**: Executive sponsorships, ride-alongs, enterprise steering committees, renewal health checks.
3. **Opportunity Assessment**: Size markets, evaluate economics, scenario plan, define go/no-go thresholds.
4. **Commercial Alignment**: Sync sales, marketing, product to ensure positioning, enablement, and delivery readiness.

## Culture & Talent Stewardship
- **Leadership Expectations**: Document leadership principles, run 360 programmes, coach direct reports, succession planning.
- **Employee Listening**: Quarterly pulse surveys, skip-level sessions, culture roundtables, action plan follow-ups.
- **Recognition & Rituals**: Company all-hands, value awards, storytelling, shared wins and learnings.
- **Org Design Governance**: Approve structure changes, spans/layers analysis, resource rebalancing, change management.

## Financial & Risk Oversight
1. **Operating Plan Review**: Partner with CFO on forecast accuracy, cash runway, investment envelope, efficiency targets.
2. **Risk Register Review**: Evaluate strategic, operational, compliance risks; ensure mitigation owners and timelines.
3. **Capital Allocation Decisions**: Approve M&A, capex, hiring plans, pricing moves grounded in scenario analysis.
4. **Policy & Compliance**: Ensure ESG commitments, ethics hotline follow-up, regulatory relationships, audit readiness.

## Execution & Accountability Framework
- **Goal Cascading**: Translate strategy to company/department OKRs, track alignment, resolve conflicts.
- **Operating Mechanisms**: Maintain single source of truth dashboard, exec action logs, follow-through cadences.
- **Decision Records**: Document pivotal calls, rationale, and review checkpoints; revisit assumptions on cadence.
- **Postmortems & Learnings**: Sponsor business postmortems, capture lessons, integrate into playbooks.

## Eric's Leadership Routines
- Monday: Review dashboards, meet Chief of Staff, align priorities.
- Midweek: Customer engagements, media/analyst interactions, investor updates.
- Thursday: Deep work on strategic initiatives, product/tech partner sync.
- Friday: ELT retro, culture touchpoints, written recap to the company.
