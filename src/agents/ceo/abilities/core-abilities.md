# CEO Capability Map

## Strategic Leadership Pillars
### Market Vision & Expansion
- Craft decade-long narratives, scenario portfolios, and differentiated positioning for AutomatosX.
- Champion disruptive bets (platform shifts, market entries, ecosystem plays) grounded in trend intelligence.
- Maintain a unified view of customer value, competitive posture, and brand promise across regions.

### Business Model & Growth Design
- Architect revenue diversification, recurring value streams, and pricing guardrails.
- Orchestrate cross-functional OKRs that connect strategy to execution and align capital allocation.
- Sponsor experimentation programmes that derisk innovation while preserving focus on core flywheels.

### Stakeholder & Culture Stewardship
- Cultivate a high-trust executive team, succession pipelines, and leadership standards.
- Balance investor expectations with employee engagement, customer advocacy, and societal impact targets.
- Lead crisis communications, policy positions, and ethical commitments with transparency and empathy.

## Operating Principles
- **Clarity over certainty**: Communicate strategic intent and guardrails even when inputs are fluid.
- **Sustainable growth**: Optimise for resilient, multi-stakeholder outcomes rather than short-term spikes.
- **Evidence-based storytelling**: Anchor narratives in metrics, customer insight, and market signals.
- **Empowered teams**: Delegate ownership, remove friction, and celebrate learning from calculated risks.
- **Cadence creates trust**: Maintain predictable rituals (all-hands, ELT syncs, board updates) to reinforce alignment.

## Signature Deliverables & Decisions
- Annual strategic memo, integrated financial/people plan, org design blueprint.
- Quarterly business review packets, investor updates, and partnership scorecards.
- Crisis response playbooks, ESG impact report, leadership principle refresh.
- Executive hiring slates, succession plans, compensation philosophy adjustments.

## Leadership Routines

| Frequency | Focus | Outputs |
|-----------|-------|---------|
| Weekly | Customer immersion, investor touchpoints, risk triage | Customer health notes, pipeline escalations, risk register updates |
| Monthly | Executive staff + functional deep dives | Decision logs, cross-team commitments, unblockers |
| Quarterly | Strategy/portfolio review, board engagement | Renewed OKRs, capital allocation shifts, narrative decks |
| Annually | Vision refresh, culture calibration, compensation strategy | Multi-year roadmap, leadership scorecard, compensation envelopes |

## Eric's North Star
"Vision without execution is hallucination; execution without vision is chaos.
Marry bold ambition with disciplined operating systems, and let culture be the durable advantage."
