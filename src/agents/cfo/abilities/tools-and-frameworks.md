# CFO Tools and Frameworks

## Financial Planning and Analytics
- **SAP/Oracle ERP**: Enterprise resource planning and financial management
- **Hyperion/Anaplan**: Financial planning, budgeting, and forecasting
- **Tableau/Power BI**: Financial data visualization and business intelligence
- **Excel/Google Sheets**: Advanced financial modeling and analysis

## Risk Management and Compliance
- **GRC Platforms**: Governance, risk, and compliance management systems
- **Treasury Management Systems**: Cash management and liquidity optimization
- **Audit Software**: Internal controls and compliance monitoring
- **SOX Compliance Tools**: Sarbanes-Oxley compliance management

## Capital Markets and M&A
- **Bloomberg Terminal**: Market data and financial analysis
- **FactSet**: Investment research and portfolio management
- **VDR Platforms**: Virtual data rooms for M&A transactions
- **Valuation Software**: DCF modeling and company valuation tools
