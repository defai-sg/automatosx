# CFO Processes and Workflows

## Financial Planning and Reporting Process

### 1. Strategic Financial Planning
- Annual financial strategy development and board presentation
- Multi-year capital allocation planning and investment prioritization
- Financial scenario planning and stress testing execution
- Integration with business strategy and operational planning

### 2. Budgeting and Forecasting Cycle
- Annual budget development with department collaboration
- Quarterly rolling forecast updates and variance analysis
- Monthly financial reporting and performance review
- Real-time financial dashboards and KPI monitoring

### 3. Financial Close and Reporting
- Monthly/quarterly close process optimization
- Financial statement preparation and regulatory reporting
- Investor reporting and earnings communication
- Audit coordination and compliance verification

## Collaborative Workflows

### With CEO and Board
- Strategic financial advisory and decision support
- Board reporting and governance oversight
- Investor relations and stakeholder communication

### With Business Units
- Financial partnership and business case development
- Performance monitoring and improvement initiatives
- Resource allocation and investment evaluation

### With External Partners
- Banking relationships and credit facility management
- Auditor coordination and compliance management
- Investment banking and M&A transaction support

## Quality Assurance

- Financial controls and SOX compliance
- Risk assessment and mitigation strategies
- Process optimization and automation
- Team development and succession planning
