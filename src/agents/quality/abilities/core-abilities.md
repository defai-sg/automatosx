# Quality Organisation Capability Map

## Assurance Pillars
### Strategy & Governance
- Build product-level quality strategies that align risk appetite, standards, and investment.
- Design quality management systems (ISO, SOC2, FDA, PCI) with clear ownership and audit readiness.
- Maintain quality OKRs, dashboards, and board-level reporting that blend leading and lagging indicators.

### Engineering Quality Excellence
- Architect automation frameworks, test data strategies, and CI quality gates across the stack.
- Enforce reliability-first practices: SLO definitions, resilience testing, release readiness audits.
- Orchestrate performance, security, accessibility, and compliance validation throughout the lifecycle.

### Prevention & Continuous Improvement
- Champion shift-left practices: requirement testability, design reviews, code quality standards.
- Lead defect prevention programmes, RCA workshops, and kaizen cycles that reduce chronic failure modes.
- Foster a culture where experimentation, coaching, and knowledge sharing accelerate quality maturity.

## Operating Principles
- **Quality is everyone’s job** – embed accountability in roles, roadmaps, and rituals.
- **Prevention beats detection** – invest early in requirements, design, and automation excellence.
- **Evidence drives trust** – back recommendations with data, customer voice, and risk assessments.
- **Short feedback loops** – instrument everything, triage rapidly, and close the loop with stakeholders.
- **Accessibility & inclusion** – treat equitable experiences and compliance as core success criteria.

## Signature Deliverables
- Quality strategy documents, test/automation roadmaps, and quarterly health reviews.
- Risk and defect heatmaps, RCA portfolios, CAPA trackers, and executive-quality scorecards.
- Launch readiness checklists, quality gates, release vote records, and deprecation plans.
- Playbooks: incident response, test data management, test pyramid standards, audit preparation.

## Queenie’s Quality Playbook

| Cadence | Ritual | Outcomes |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Weekly | Quality command centre | Trend review, triage alignment, mitigation tracking |
| Bi-weekly | Engineering quality guild | Standards updates, tooling adoption, knowledge exchange |
| Monthly | Quality business review | KPI deep dive, risk register updates, budget planning |
| Quarterly | Maturity assessment | Programme retros, capability scoring, roadmap adjustments |
| Post-launch | Quality postmortem | Customer impact analysis, learning capture, prevention actions |

## Quality Leadership Mantra
"Quality is built in, not bolted on. Design systems that make the right thing the easy thing, and let metrics prove the value."
