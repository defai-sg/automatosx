# CTO Processes and Workflows

## Strategic Planning & Execution Cadence
1. **Annual Vision Cycle**: Define technology pillars, capability targets, investment theses, and measurable outcomes.
2. **Quarterly Portfolio Review**: Rebalance initiatives, assess ROI, adjust funding, align with company OKRs.
3. **Roadmap Governance**: Maintain integrated platform roadmap, dependency maps, and risk registers shared with ELT.
4. **Communication Rhythm**: Publish monthly CTO notes, run architecture councils, and lead executive readouts.

## Technology Portfolio Management
- **Intake & Prioritisation**: Evaluate proposals against strategic scorecards (value, feasibility, risk, differentiation).
- **Stage-Gate Reviews**: Gate 0 (concept), Gate 1 (architecture), Gate 2 (build), Gate 3 (scale) with exit criteria.
- **Metrics & Reporting**: Track DORA/SPACE, platform adoption, error budgets, FinOps metrics, and innovation pipeline.
- **Vendor Lifecycle**: Run RFPs, negotiate MSAs, set SLA guardrails, execute quarterly business reviews.

## Engineering Operations & Reliability
1. **Platform Enablement**: Maintain internal developer platform, golden paths, shared services backlog.
2. **Delivery Excellence**: Enforce change management policy, progressive delivery, branch strategy, observability standards.
3. **Reliability Reviews**: Weekly SLO health checks, incident trend analysis, risk remediation tracking with SRE.
4. **Security & Compliance**: Chair risk council, oversee audits, ensure policy-as-code and continuous compliance monitors.

## Org & Talent Development Cycle
- **Workforce Planning**: Forecast headcount, skills gaps, succession, and geographic distribution each quarter.
- **Performance & Growth**: Run calibration sessions, leadership coaching, technical ladders, compensation alignment.
- **Culture & Engagement**: Execute pulse surveys, retros on operating model, hack weeks, and recognition programmes.
- **Hiring Operations**: Partner with TA on scorecards, diversity goals, structured interviews, closing narratives.

## Stakeholder & External Engagement
- **Executive Partnerships**: Weekly triad with CEO/CPO, finance syncs on budget, legal/compliance check-ins.
- **Board Engagement**: Prepare quarterly narrative decks, risk assessments, customer wins, technology differentiators.
- **Customer Councils**: Meet strategic customers for roadmap validation, co-innovation, and executive escalations.
- **Public Advocacy**: Manage analyst briefings, conference speaking, open-source strategy, and community investments.

## Incident Command & Crisis Response
1. **Preparedness**: Maintain incident playbooks, escalation matrices, and tabletop exercises.
2. **Response**: Assume executive sponsor role, communicate status, unblock teams, manage stakeholder expectations.
3. **Recovery**: Validate resolution, coordinate customer updates, oversee regulatory obligations.
4. **Postmortem**: Sponsor blameless review, approve remediation investments, update governance artefacts.

## Continuous Improvement & Innovation
- **Technology Radar**: Semi-annual review of tools, frameworks, and deprecations with architecture guild.
- **Process Retrospectives**: Evaluate operating model effectiveness, pipeline efficiency, tooling ROI.
- **Innovation Budgeting**: Allocate experimentation fund, track outcomes, scale successful pilots.
- **Knowledge Sharing**: Host CTO forums, publish playbooks, run leadership summits, maintain internal knowledge base.
