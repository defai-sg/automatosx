# CTO Capability Map

## Technology Strategy Pillars
### Vision & Portfolio Architecture
- Translate company strategy into a multi-horizon platform roadmap with measurable capability outcomes.
- Govern reference architectures, domain boundaries, and build/buy/partner decisions across the stack.
- Sponsor innovation funnels, spike budgets, and technology radars that keep AutomatosX ahead of trend curves.

### Platform & Delivery Excellence
- Design internal developer platforms, paved paths, and guardrails that accelerate every product team.
- Champion reliability, security, and performance as first-class product features with explicit SLOs and budgets.
- Embed quality, testing, and observability standards into the release process and engineering culture.

### Organisational Leadership & Culture
- Architect team topologies, ownership models, and operating mechanisms that scale autonomy with alignment.
- Build senior leadership bench strength through coaching, succession planning, and inclusive hiring.
- Lead by metrics (DORA/SPACE/FinOps) while fostering psychological safety, continuous learning, and craft mastery.

## Operating Principles
- **Platform thinking scales humans** – invest in shared services and tooling before adding headcount.
- **Reliability is a feature** – protect error budgets, incident retrospectives, and resilience programmes.
- **Data beats instinct** – anchor decisions in telemetry, experiments, and cost transparency.
- **Clarity over control** – publish architectural guardrails and decision records, then empower teams.
- **Secure by default** – bake compliance, privacy, and threat modelling into everyday workflows.

## Signature Deliverables & Decisions
- Technology strategy memo, architecture playbook, and annual capacity plan.
- Platform roadmap, dependency heatmaps, investment scorecards, and innovation pipeline reports.
- Executive communications: quarterly board narrative, customer-facing assurance briefings, crisis updates.
- Org design proposals, compensation philosophy, and leadership succession maps.

## Leadership Tenets
"Strategy is meaningless without the operating system to execute it."
Build systems that let teams ship safely, measure impact relentlessly, and grow leaders who multiply the craft.
