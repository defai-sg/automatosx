# Universal Operating Rhythms

## Delivery Lifecycle (Discover → Deliver → Learn)
1. **Discover**: Define the problem, stakeholders, success metrics, and constraints; surface assumptions and risks.
2. **Frame & Plan**: Produce a lightweight brief, align timelines, sequence dependencies, and secure approvals.
3. **Execute**: Build incrementally, maintain async status updates, protect quality gates, and keep scope transparent.
4. **Release & Measure**: Run launch checklist, communicate changes, validate impact against KPIs, monitor health.
5. **Learn & Adapt**: Facilitate retrospective, capture decisions, update playbooks, feed insights into next cycle.

## Collaboration Rituals
- **Daily Async Check-ins**: Posted updates (Done / Doing / Blocked) with links to work items and decisions.
- **Weekly Syncs**: Focus on roadmap shifts, risk mitigation, and cross-functional dependencies.
- **Demo/Showcase Cadence**: Share progress, gather feedback, celebrate incremental wins.
- **Retrospectives**: Inspect process health, psychological safety, tooling needs, and action items.

## Quality & Risk Controls
- **Definition of Ready**: Clarity on problem, acceptance criteria, data/monitoring plan, stakeholders on call.
- **Definition of Done**: Tests passing, documentation updated, observability configured, communication shipped.
- **Risk Registers**: Maintain top risks, mitigation owners, review cadence integrated into planning.
- **Change Management**: Announce changes via release notes, internal channels, and support enablement.

## Continuous Improvement Loop
- Track leading (cycle time, quality signals) and lagging (outcomes, adoption) indicators.
- Schedule monthly process audits: identify bottlenecks, automation opportunities, or training needs.
- Encourage experiment days/hack sprints to evolve tooling, templates, and team rituals.
- Archive learnings in the global knowledge base with owners, review dates, and applicability tags.

## Culture & Inclusion Practices
- Use rotating facilitators, meeting-free focus blocks, and documented decisions to support distributed work.
- Pair new collaborators with buddies, maintain onboarding checklists, and host office hours.
- Celebrate wins, highlight learnings, and support wellness through sustainable workloads.

## Escalation & Support Pathways

| Scenario | First Response | Next Step |
|----------|----------------|-----------|
| Delivery risk | Async escalation in project channel with context | Schedule unblock session, adjust plan |
| Incident | Trigger on-call/incident bot | Follow playbook, communicate status updates |
| Decision conflict | Document options + trade-offs | Bring to weekly sync / exec sponsor |
| Scope change | Update brief + stakeholders | Re-run impact analysis, adjust commitments |

## Global Mindset Reminder
Shared processes are scaffolding, not bureaucracy. Use them to create clarity, protect quality, and give every team the same chance to succeed.
