# Product Design Processes and Workflows

## Double Diamond Delivery Rhythm
1. **Discover**: Kickoff with research plan, mixed-method studies, competitive teardown, analytics review, and hypothesis log.
2. **Define**: Synthesize insights, produce opportunity solution trees, prioritise jobs-to-be-done, align success metrics.
3. **Develop**: Rapid concept sketching, co-design with engineering, iterative prototyping (low → high fidelity), usability loops.
4. **Deliver**: Final UI polish, spec handoff, asset packaging, QA support, design QA verification in staging.

## Continuous Research Lifecycle
- **Research Backlog**: Maintain prioritized questions, link to product OKRs, ensure ongoing recruitment pipeline.
- **Study Execution**: Conduct moderated/unmoderated sessions, card sorts, diary studies, analytics deep dives.
- **Insight Management**: Tag findings in Dovetail/Aurelius, surface patterns, drive quarterly research readouts.
- **Impact Tracking**: Tie insights to roadmap decisions, adoption metrics, and user satisfaction deltas.

## Design System & Ops Workflow
1. **Intake**: Capture component requests, rationale, and intended usage.
2. **Design & Review**: Prototype in Figma, validate accessibility, capture usage guidelines.
3. **Build & Test**: Partner with frontend guild, implement in Storybook, run visual regression + a11y tests.
4. **Rollout**: Update tokens, publish documentation, communicate change log, migrate legacy patterns.
5. **Lifecycle**: Track adoption, sunset deprecated components, archive learnings.

## Cross-Functional Collaboration Cadence
- **Weekly Product Trio Sync**: Designer, PM, Tech Lead align on discovery findings, prioritisation, and scope trade-offs.
- **Design Critiques**: Structured feedback sessions with peer designers to stress-test flows, microcopy, and motion.
- **Engineering Pairing**: Inspect component feasibility, performance implications, and implementation approach.
- **Go-to-Market Alignment**: Brief marketing/support on UX changes, create release assets, update help center content.

## Quality & Accessibility Safeguards
- **Design QA Checklist**: Token usage, spacing, responsiveness, state coverage, empty/error/loading states.
- **Accessibility Verification**: Contrast validation, semantic markup guidance, alternative interaction patterns.
- **Prototype Testing**: Pre-build validation with target users, measuring task completion, satisfaction, and effort.
- **Post-Launch Monitoring**: Review heatmaps, support tickets, analytics, and user feedback to iterate quickly.

## Continuous Improvement & Craft Growth
- **Retrospectives**: Monthly reflections on process friction, tooling gaps, and collaborative health.
- **Learning Sprints**: Allocate time for trend scans, new tooling experiments, conference debriefs.
- **DesignOps Metrics**: Track cycle time, review turnaround, design debt, and system adoption.
- **Knowledge Sharing**: Maintain design playbook, pattern library rationale, recorded workshops, and onboarding guides.
